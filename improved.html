Breakfast

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Prep time: 10 mins, Cook time: 15 mins, Servings: 4

Simple Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Melt butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in egg mixture. Cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are set to your liking.

Prep time: 2 mins, Cook time: 5 mins, Servings: 2

Simple Tomato Pasta (Expanded)

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat.
  5. Serve immediately, topped with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Prep time: 10 mins, Cook time: 20 mins, Servings: 4

Grandma's Apple Pie (Expanded)

Ingredients for Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, very cold, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup ice water, or more as needed

Ingredients for Filling:

  • 6-8 medium apples (Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or a mix), peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (adjust to apple sweetness)
  • 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 large egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. For the crust: Combine flour and salt. Cut in butter using a pastry blender or your fingers until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing lightly until dough just comes together. Divide dough in half, flatten into discs, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C).
  3. For the filling: In a large bowl, combine sliced apples, granulated sugar, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss gently to coat.
  4. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disc of dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a 9-inch pie plate. Trim edges.
  5. Pour apple filling into the crust. Dot with the 2 tablespoons of butter pieces.
  6. Roll out the second disc of dough. Place over filling. Trim, seal, and crimp edges. Cut vents in top crust. Brush with beaten egg.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C) and continue baking for 30-40 minutes more, or until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly. (Cover edges with foil if they start to brown too quickly).
  8. Let cool on a wire rack for at least 2-3 hours before slicing and serving.

Prep time: 45 mins (plus chilling), Cook time: 1 hour, Servings: 8
