üí´ Classic Deviled Eggs
Ingredients:
  • 12 eggs
  • 3 tsp dijon mustard
  • ‚Öì cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp minced shallot / onion
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ‚Öõ tsp salt
  • ‚Öõ tsp pepper
  • ¬º tsp tabasco sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • paprika + chives + pickle juice
 Instructions:
  1. Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with water.
  2. Transfer to stovetop over high heat until water begins to boil.
  3. Boil for one minute, cover with lid, and remove from heat. Allow to sit for 17 minutes and then drain and transfer to an ice bath.
  4. Peel eggs and set aside.
  5. Slice each egg in half lengthwise and gently remove yolk halves into small mixing bowl. Arrange egg whites on serving platter.
  6. Use a fork to mash the yolks together with mustard, mayonnaise, onion, tabasco, salt, and pepper.
  7. Add mayonnaise as necessary to get desired creamy consistency, then piping bag or spoon mixture back into egg halves.
  8. Sprinkle with paprika and let settle.

üí´ No-Knead Dutch Oven Bread
Ingredients:
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1¬Ω cups very warm tap water
  • 3 cups bread flour
 Instructions:
  1. Whisk yeast and salt in a large bowl with warm water.
  2. Add in all the flour at once and stir until a sticky dough forms.
  3. Cover with a kitchen towel and leave for 2-3 hours to rise until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 450¬∞F with Dutch oven inside and lid on.
  5. Dust dough with flour and form it into a ball on parchment paper.
  6. Lift paper and dough into Dutch oven and return to oven.
  7. Bake 30 minutes covered, then 12 minutes uncovered.
  8. Remove and place on cooling rack for at least 10 minutes before slicing.

üí´ Ultra-Crispy Roast Potatoes
Ingredients:
  • 2 liters water
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • ¬Ω tsp baking soda
  • 4 lbs Yukon Gold or Russet potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 5 tbsp olive oil or animal fat
  • Small handful chopped fresh rosemary leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Black pepper
  • Small handful minced fresh parsley leaves
 Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 450¬∞F.
  2. Boil water in a large pot, then add salt, baking soda and potatoes.
  3. Return to boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes or until potatoes are easily pierced with a knife.
  4. In a saucepan, combine oil/fat, rosemary, garlic and pepper. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until garlic is golden (about 3 minutes).
  5. Strain oil through a fine mesh into a large bowl, retaining both parts.
  6. Drain potatoes and let rest in pot for moisture to evaporate.
  7. Transfer potatoes to bowl with infused oil, season with salt and pepper, and shake to coat and create a thick paste.
  8. Spread potatoes evenly on a baking sheet and roast undisturbed for 20 minutes.
  9. Remove from oven, turn potatoes with a spatula and continue roasting for 35 minutes, turning and shaking occasionally.
  10. Transfer potatoes to a bowl, add herbs (including parsley) and toss to coat. Serve.
