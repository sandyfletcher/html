  2. Add in all the flour at once and stir until a sticky dough forms.
  3. Cover with a kitchen towel and leave for 2-3 hours to rise until doubled in size.

Pizza Dough
  • 1 cup water, warmed
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  1. Combine water, yeast, and sugar in medium bowl. Let stand until the mixture foams on top - about 5 minutes.
  2. In the meantime, combine flour and salt. Once yeast mixture is foamy, mix it into the flour mixture.
  3. Knead combination on floured surface, then place in lightly oiled bowl and cover with a clean cloth.
  4. Let the dough rise - about 30 minutes - then cut the dough in half.
  5. Use or bag each portion for later use.
  6. Pizza Stone
  7. Preheat oven to 500¬∞F.
  8. Place stone on the lowest rack for 30 minutes while preheating.
  9. Dust stone with flour before placing pizza on stone.

Deviled Eggs
  • 12 eggs
  • 3 tsp dijon mustard
  • ‚Öì cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp minced shallot / onion
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ‚Öõ tsp salt
  • ‚Öõ tsp pepper
  • ¬º tsp tabasco sauce
  • Paprika, chives, pickle juice (for garnish/flavor)
  1. Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with water.
  2. Transfer to stovetop over high heat until water begins to boil.
  3. Boil for one minute, cover with lid, and remove from heat. Allow to sit for 17 minutes and then drain and transfer to an ice bath.
  4. Peel eggs and set aside.
  5. Slice each egg in half lengthwise and gently remove yolk halves into small mixing bowl. Arrange egg whites on serving platter.
  6. Use a fork to mash the yolks together with mustard, mayonnaise, butter, lemon juice, shallot/onion, sugar, salt, pepper, and tabasco.
  7. Add mayonnaise as necessary to get desired creamy consistency, then piping bag or spoon mixture back into egg halves.
  8. Sprinkle with paprika and chives. Add a dash of pickle juice if desired. Let settle.
