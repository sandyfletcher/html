Breakfast Specials

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Prep time: 10 mins, Cook time: 15 mins, Servings: 4

Simple Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Melt butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in egg mixture. Cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are set to your liking.

Prep time: 2 mins, Cook time: 5 mins, Servings: 2

Lunchtime Favorites

Classic Tuna Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (5 ounces) tuna, drained
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon chopped celery (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Lettuce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix tuna, mayonnaise, celery, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread tuna mixture on 2 slices of bread.
  3. Top with lettuce if desired, and the remaining slices of bread.

Prep time: 5 mins, Cook time: 0 mins, Servings: 2

Delicious Dinners

Simple Tomato Pasta (Expanded)

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat.
  5. Serve immediately, topped with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Prep time: 10 mins, Cook time: 20 mins, Servings: 4

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
